Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 25, 2025

Lint Notes (“the app”, “we”, “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our app.

Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or share personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, or account details.

All notes created using Lint Notes are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, access, analyze, or share your notes.

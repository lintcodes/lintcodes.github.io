Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25 Dec 2025

Lint Notes (“the app”, “we”, “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or contacts.

Notes & Content

All notes created in the app are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, access, or share your notes in any form.

2. Permissions

Lint Notes may request certain permissions only to provide core functionality:

  • Storage (optional): To save notes locally on your device
  • Internet (optional): Used only for ads or app updates, if enabled

We do not access data unrelated to the app’s functionality.

3. Advertising

If ads are enabled in the app, they may be provided by third-party services such as Google AdMob.

These services may collect anonymous, non-personal information (such as device information or advertising identifiers) to display relevant ads.

We do not control how third-party advertisers collect or use data.

You can review Google’s Privacy Policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data. Since all notes are stored locally on your device, we do not maintain any servers containing your personal data.

However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

5. Children’s Privacy

Lint Notes is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app or its store listing.

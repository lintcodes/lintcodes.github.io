Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2, 2025

Day Counter ("the App") helps you track days to or since events and optionally configure a widget and reminders. We care about your privacy and strive to collect as little data as possible.

Information We Collect

  • Analytics: The App uses Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google LLC, to understand how users interact with the app and to improve performance and user experience.

    Firebase Analytics may collect non-personal information such as:

    • App usage data (screens viewed, interactions)
    • Device information (device model, OS version)
    • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
    • App instance identifiers

    This data is collected in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

    Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

  • Ads: The App uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and approximate location (via IP) to deliver and measure ads. See Google’s Advertising Policies.
  • Diagnostics: Google services may collect basic diagnostics (e.g., crash logs, performance data) to improve stability.

The App does not require you to create an account and does not collect your name, email address, or precise location.

How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App’s features
  • Deliver, measure, and improve advertising (via AdMob)
  • Ensure performance and reliability

Third‑Party Services

The App uses the following third‑party services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Services: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Data Retention

Event data you create (names, dates, and options) are stored locally on your device. Advertising and diagnostic data are retained by Google according to their policies.

Your Choices

  • You can limit ad personalization in your device settings (Google settings > Ads).
  • You can remove the App to stop all data collection by it.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age where parental consent is required under applicable law. If you believe a child has provided data in violation of this policy, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date above and include the new policy in app updates.

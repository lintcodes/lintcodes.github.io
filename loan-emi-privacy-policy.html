Loan EMI Calculator — Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 11, 2026

The Loan EMI Calculator app helps you calculate loan EMIs, view amortization schedules, and use financial calculators for FD, RD, PPF, and NPS. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information Collection & Usage

  • All calculations and data remain on your device. We do not access, store, or transmit any user data to our servers or third parties.
  • The app does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.
